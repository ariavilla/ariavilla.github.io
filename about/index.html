About | 关于我

GONG Ke, Aria 宫珂

  • 《体坛周报》、体坛加综合部编辑与记者，负责网球、体操以及冰雪项目撰稿。
  • 从2004年雅典奥运会开始就是一位体育迷，现在把爱好变成了事业——有兴趣了解这段经历吗？
  • 虽然日常工作撰写的主要稿件都与竞技有关，但更愿意超越竞技，触及体育行业与相关人物背后的故事。
  • 在中国人民大学历史学院完成本科学业，在香港中文大学新闻与传播学院完成硕士学业。
  • 当然，认为自己是一个女权主义者，同样也更愿意关注体育行业中女性以及性少数群体的议题。
  • 除了体育之外，还对这些事情感兴趣：艺术史、当代艺术、粤语流行乐、交叉训练与莱美课程、发现北京的宝藏去处、独自旅行。
  • 现阶段仍然在努力的事情：提高 iPhone 摄影技术、重新开始学西班牙语、拿到第一个莱美教练证。
  • 未来希望探索更多的事情：有关体育的一切、跨文化沟通、数字营销。
  • Works as editor and journalist at Titan sports, specialized in tennis, gymnastics, and winter sports.
  • Has been a sports enthusiast for 16 years (and counting) and turns her hobby into career.
  • Is always looking to go beyond competitive level of sports and discover untold stories within sports industry.
  • Was academically trained in School of History, Renmin University of China (B.A.) and School of Journalism and Communication, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (M.S.).
  • Identifies herself a feminist, of course.
  • Also has strong passion for: art history, contemporary art, Cantonese pop music, cross training and Les Mills classes, discovering hidden gems in Beijing, and solo traveling.
  • Is still working on: iPhone photography skills, resuming Spanish learning, and getting a Les Mills instructor certificate.
  • Would like to explore more on: all things sports industry, cross-culture communication, and digital marketing.
